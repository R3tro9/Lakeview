Frequently asked questions

How do I add my existing devices to Lakeview?
You can add devices manually one at a time using the Add Device screen. On the Pro plan and above, you can import directly from Google Admin to pull your entire managed fleet in automatically.
Which ticket systems does Lakeview support?
Lakeview integrates with Autotask (Datto), ServiceNow, Jira Service Desk, Freshservice, and Zendesk. When you log a repair, Lakeview auto-generates the ticket link so it's always one click away.
How does team access work?
The Administrator who sets up the account can invite teammates by email. Invited users sign in with their Google account. Administrators have full access; Technicians can look up devices and log repairs.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Each organization's data is completely isolated — no other Lakeview customer can see your devices or repair records. All data is stored in Supabase with row-level security enforced at the database level.
Can I cancel my plan?
Absolutely. Cancel any time from your account settings with no cancellation fees. Your data remains accessible until the end of your billing period.
What counts as a device?
Any asset tracked in Lakeview counts toward your device limit — laptops, desktops, Chromebooks, tablets, phones, printers, and network equipment.
